Hyperion.ai Service Shutdown Notice

Hello! Thank you for your interest in Hyperion.ai. Unfortunately, Hyperion.ai the Hyperion.ai service is currently in the process of being shut down and is not accepting any new registrations or downloads. Hyperion Industries no longer has the resources to continue operating and supporting the service to our standards so we have made the difficult decision to shut down the service.

If you are a current user, we have included some details in the following section on how to migrate your data off of Hyperion.ai.

Next Steps for Current Users

Final Day

Current users will be able to use the app until July 7, 2017. After which the servers will officially be taken down and become inaccessible from the app.

Exporting Data before October 7, 2017

To export your data at any time before October 7, 2017, current users can follow the following steps to export their data:

  1. Login at https://id.hyperion.industries
  2. Click on the "Settings" link in the top left.
  3. Select "Export Data" and follow the remaining steps that appear.

Exporting Data before March 10, 2018

Hyperion Industries will restain an offline copy of the database up until March 10, 2018. If you have missed the October 7 cutoff, send an email to alex@hyperionindustries.io with "Data Export Request" as the Subject. Please make sure you use the same email you used to sign up to the service.

Data Format

Your data will be exported as a CSV that conforms to the RFC 4180 specification proposal.

Thank You!

Hyperion Industries apologies for any inconvinience this may cause. We hope the app has made a positive impact in your life and are incredibly grateful for the reception and feedback. Stay tuned for future projects!

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to send an email to alex@hyperionindustries.io
